Blackjack Rules

Objective: Get a hand value equal or closest to 21 without going over.

Numbered cards are worth their face value.

Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10 points.

Ace can be worth either 1 or 11 points.

If your hand value exceeds 21, you are considered "bust" and lose the game.
